What Is Canopy Lifting?

Canopy lifting, also known as crown lifting, is a tree pruning technique that involves the removal of lower branches to raise the height of the tree's canopy. This process enhances clearance beneath the tree, improving accessibility, safety, and aesthetics.

Why Is Canopy Lifting Necessary?

  • Improved Safety – Eliminates hazards from low-hanging branches
  • Enhanced Visibility – Better sightlines for traffic and security
  • Better Airflow and Light Penetration
  • Aesthetic Appeal – Creates a cleaner, structured appearance
