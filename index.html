Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do your arborists have?

All our arborists are certified by the International Society of Arboriculture (ISA) and have extensive experience in tree care. We maintain ongoing training to stay current with the latest industry standards and safety protocols, including compliance with Australian regulations. Our team is fully licensed and insured.

Do you provide emergency tree services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency tree services for storm damage, fallen trees, and other urgent situations. Our rapid response team is always ready to handle any tree-related emergency safely and efficiently.

What areas in Brisbane do you service?

We provide arborist services across all of Brisbane and surrounding suburbs. Whether you’re in the inner city, north, south, east, or west, our team is equipped to assist you.

How do I know if my tree needs to be removed?

Our certified arborists can assess your tree’s health and safety. Common reasons for removal include disease, structural instability, or if the tree poses a risk to your property or family. We always prioritize safety and will recommend the best course of action.

Do you offer stump grinding services?

Yes, we provide stump grinding services to completely remove unsightly or hazardous stumps from your property. Our equipment is designed to grind stumps below ground level, allowing you to replant or landscape the area.

Are your services insured?

Absolutely. We are fully insured, including public liability and workers’ compensation, to protect you and your property during our services. You can have peace of mind knowing you’re in safe hands.

Can you help with council permits for tree removal?

Yes, we can assist with navigating Brisbane City Council regulations and obtaining any necessary permits for tree removal or pruning. Our team is familiar with local laws and can guide you through the process.

What is the cost of tree removal?

The cost of tree removal depends on factors such as the size, location, and condition of the tree. We offer free, no-obligation quotes tailored to your specific needs. Contact us today for an assessment.

Do you offer regular tree maintenance services?

Yes, we provide ongoing tree maintenance services, including pruning, trimming, and health assessments. Regular maintenance helps keep your trees healthy, safe, and looking their best.

How do I book your services?

You can book our services by calling us directly or filling out the contact form on our website. Our team will respond promptly to schedule a convenient time for your service.
